This privacy policy applies to Pomozen, a free and open-source Pomodoro timer app developed by Arun (arunuserx@gmail.com) & Abilash S.

Information Collection

Pomozen does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any data externally.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or services that access personal data.

Analytics

No analytics or tracking systems are integrated. Your activity remains private and untracked.

Children's Privacy

Pomozen is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data, making it safe for children to use.
